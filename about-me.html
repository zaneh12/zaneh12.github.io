About Me

Research

  • Primarily my research is in scoring and aggregating forecasts of time-critical events. This is applied to events like football & tennis matches, and also more disperate questions related to geopolitics (see Metaculus)
  • The research's goal is to identify novel and statistically rigorous methods for achieving these goals, while keeping the models interpretable
  • For this reason I don't work with any Machine Learning models like Neural Networks or Gradient Boosting Machines for prediction and instead try and use methods such as first-hitting-time of Wiener Processes or variations of Poisson Races
